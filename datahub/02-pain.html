Chapter 01 — The Problem

Sound familiar?

Think about the last time a data quality issue significantly impacted your team. These aren't edge cases — they're happening right now, in dozens of places.

Campaign to wrong contacts
Finance report rebuilt from scratch
AI output nobody trusted
📧
Campaign failure
A campaign delivered to the wrong contacts.
The CRM had one version of the list. The marketing platform had another. Nobody caught the divergence before send. The damage was done before IT knew there was a problem.
📊
Reporting failure
A finance report rebuilt because the CRM and ERP didn't agree.
Two systems. Two versions of the same customer record. Three days of analyst time reconciling which one was right — and still no certainty about the answer.
🤖
AI failure
An AI output someone printed — and then set aside.
The model was fine. The data it ran on wasn't. Nobody could say with confidence where the inputs came from or whether they'd been validated. So nobody acted on it.
💡
That cycle — find it, trace it, fix it — is happening right now, in dozens of places. This is a data problem. And the data problem is an integration problem.
